KnowThis
Writer, editor & producer · NowThis

At NowThis, I wrote, edited, and produced our daily flagship newsletter, KnowThis, which summarized the day's top five stories in a native mobile format. In my six months of producing the newsletter full-time, I increased our open rate by 4% — exceeding the industry standard — leading to the product's best-ever open rate during the summer of 2020 in its year-plus of existence. Prior to writing the newsletter full-time, I edited and wrote it interchangeably. In that process, I developed a new, streamlined workflow by working with insights, publishing and editorial teams.
